Gaze and Driver Fatigue Detection

Aditya Chechani
Nidhi Tiwari
Sept. 19, 2018

Problem Definition:

Driver monitoring & analytics is a growing area of research in computer vision and is widely regarded as an effective tool in checking the high rates of mortality that are associated with accidents that occur on roads majoritarily due to driver inattentiveness. Our attempt is to use computer vision techniques to detect signs of fatigue in drivers.

Input:

The input datasets contains face images with 70 facial feature annotations in text files.
The dataset contains augmented mirrored images as well as seen on the one on the right

Work Done:

  • Multiple Face Detection and its bounding boxes


  • 68 point facial landmark detection using dlib library


  • Trained ensemble of trees model for 70 point facial landmark detection which detects the pupils in the eyes as well
